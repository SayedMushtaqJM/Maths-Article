Mathematics in Civil Engineering

Civil engineering is a profession that applies mathematical and physical science principles to design and develop structures and methods to utilize materials and forces of nature for the benefit of humanity. Civil engineers create buildings, industrial plants and transportation infrastructure. Today, civil engineering is often combined with environmental engineering as cities and countries place more focus on sustainable building and protecting infrastructure from damage caused by natural disasters.
